Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Mar 7, 2025

This policy explains how DeepOCAT ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

When you interact with our App, we may gather the following types of information:

  • Device Data: Information such as your device ID, device name, operating system version, app version, and language preferences.
  • Usage Data: Details about your interactions with the App, including feature usage and navigation patterns.
  • Account Data: Your email address and verification details collected during account creation or login for authentication purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We utilize the collected data to:

  • Provide, maintain, and enhance our services
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • Send important updates and notifications about the App
  • Analyze usage trends to improve user experience
  • Identify and resolve technical issues
  • Prevent unauthorized access and fraudulent activities

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your information under the following circumstances:

  • To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • In connection with business transactions such as mergers, acquisitions, or asset transfers

Data Security

We employ appropriate security measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, accidental loss, or improper disclosure. Our security practices include encrypted communications and advanced verification methods.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, such as accessing, correcting, or requesting deletion. Please contact us to exercise these rights or for more information.

Policy Changes

We may periodically update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here along with the revised date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or your personal data, please reach out to us at support@deepocat.com.
